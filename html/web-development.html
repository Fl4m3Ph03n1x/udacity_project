Learn all about Web Development with the creator of Reddit! Steve Huffman tutors you through a series of lessons about how to make web applications and what he learned!

Have you ever heard of reddit? If you have not, now is a wonderful time to discover it! If you have, then by now you know that it is one of the biggest websites in the internet, with millions of views every month!

But did you know Steve Huffman? This guy is one of the co-founders of reddit, and he sure knows what he talks about!

This course teaches pretty much everything. From HTML, to GSQL and GAE and APIS it covers a lot of ground and teaches a lot of skills. This is no doubt an advanced course but beginners can also do it with a lot of hard work. As a master engineer, it was a true learning experience!

This course was one of the longest and hardest I have ever done in Udacity. It is also my favourite by far. With superb quizzes, material and a tutor that transpires experience in the web development area each homework was a well thought challenge and a true investment of my time. I truly recommend it!
